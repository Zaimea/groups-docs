## What to Include When Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

1. **Account Information**
   - Email address associated with your account
   - Group name (if applicable)

2. **Issue Details**
   - Clear description of the problem
   - Steps to reproduce the issue
   - Screenshots or screen recordings
   - Error messages (if any)

3. **Technical Information**
   - Browser and version
   - Operating system
   - Device type (desktop/mobile)

## Emergency Support

For critical issues affecting your entire team's access to Zaimea Groups, please mark your email as **URGENT** in the subject line.

> {warning} Only use URGENT for truly critical issues that prevent your team from working. Misuse may result in delayed responses.

## Feature Requests

We welcome and encourage feature requests! When suggesting a new feature:

1. **Describe the problem** you're trying to solve
2. **Explain your proposed solution**
3. **Provide use cases** or examples
4. **Mention any alternatives** you've considered

All feature requests are reviewed by our product team and added to our development roadmap based on user demand and feasibility.
