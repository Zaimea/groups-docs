## Free Plan

**Perfect for individuals and small teams getting started with time tracking.**

### What's included:
- **Basic Time Tracking**: Start/end times, breaks, notes
- **Simple Project Tracking**: Basic project association
- **Monthly Calculations**: Automatic hour totals
- **PDF Reports**: Limited monthly exports
- **Calendar View**: Interactive time entry
- **Core Plugins**: Charts and Templates
- **Basic Roles**: Owner and Member roles
- **1 Group**: Create one team/company group

### Limitations:
- Maximum 3 users
- Maximum 5 projects
- 1 GB storage limit
- No client portal
- No approval workflows
- Community support only

## Professional Plan

**Best for growing teams needing advanced collaboration and management tools.**

### All Free features, plus:
- **Unlimited Users**: Add your entire team
- **Unlimited Groups**: Manage multiple companies/organizations
- **Advanced Tracking**: All 3 tracking modes (Simple, Project, Project & Task)
- **Full Role System**: All 7 predefined roles + custom role creation
- **All Plugins Enabled**:
  - Client Portal for external collaboration
  - Report Approval workflows
  - Monthly Quotas for target tracking
  - Record Locking with cron schedules
  - Smart Templates with required fields
  - Advanced Charts and analytics
- **Priority Support**: Faster email responses
- **99.9% Uptime SLA**: Reliable service guarantee
- **10 GB Storage**: Plenty of space for your data

## Enterprise Plan

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try before I pay?
Absolutely! You can start with our **Free plan** immediately without any payment information. For the Professional features, we offer a **30-day free trial** with no credit card required.

### How does billing work?
- **Free**: No billing, no credit card required
- **Professional**: Monthly or annual billing per user
- **Enterprise**: Custom billing, typically annual

### What happens if I exceed Free plan limits?
If you exceed the 3-user or 5-project limit on the Free plan, you'll need to upgrade to continue growing. We'll notify you before you hit these limits.

### Can I switch plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

### Do you offer discounts?
We offer discounts for:
- **Annual payments** (save 20% compared to monthly)
- **Non-profit organizations** (50% discount)
- **Educational institutions** (special pricing available)

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan. You only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For Professional plans, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual Professional plan subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll issue a full refund.
